Why I'm interested in learning about it

  • It can't hurt to have at least a few of the techniques on hand, even if I think I prefer the "older" way of doing certain things in certain cases.
  • It's kind of fun.

Priority?

Eh. Depends on what I'm doing. Not critical for day-to-day life but nice to try to touch base with it now and then. Especially if it means I have a better chance of "thinking in code" when I might othewise not.
