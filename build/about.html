About this site

The idea here is that it might be nice to make a website. And it might be nice to make a website without knowing exactly what the website is going to look like, or do, or be, before starting.

I've got a vague idea for a page that's about sharing random stuff I'm into at the moment, and that might give me the chance to mess around with some techniques and ideas I come up with which I can't immediately apply anywhere else.

Also, making websites is supposed to be fun, and I'm hoping this will be kind of fun, if a bit of a mess.
